DESCRIPTION

A simple salad is a fresh and light dish made with a variety of crisp vegetables, often tossed together with a flavorful dressing. You can start with a base of leafy greens like lettuce, spinach, or arugula, then add chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, and red onions for crunch and color. For extra texture, toss in some nuts, seeds, or cheese. Dress it with olive oil, lemon juice or vinegar, and season with salt and pepper. It’s easy to customize based on what ingredients you have, making it a versatile, healthy meal or side dish.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups mixed greens (such as lettuce, spinach, or arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 cup nuts (e.g., walnuts, almonds) or seeds (e.g., sunflower, pumpkin) – optional for crunch
  • 1/4 cup cheese (e.g., feta, goat cheese, or shredded cheddar) – optional
  • Olive oil (for dressing)
  • 1-2 tablespoons vinegar (balsamic, apple cider, or red wine vinegar)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard (optional, for a tangy twist)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

STEPS

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly. Slice the cucumber, chop the tomatoes, thinly slice the red onion, and grate or slice the carrot.
  2. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and carrot. Add any optional ingredients like nuts, seeds, cheese, or avocado if desired.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, mustard (if using), salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  4. Toss and serve: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything together until evenly coated. If you prefer, serve the dressing on the side
  5. Garnish: Add any final touches like fresh herbs or croutons if desired.

Your fresh, simple salad is ready to enjoy!
